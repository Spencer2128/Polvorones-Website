UBE Polvoron

Polvoron is one of the most popular baked treats to give as pasalubong or gift. It gets its sweet, buttery melt-in-your-mouth nature from the unique combination of toasted flour, sugar, creamy butter, and powdered milk. Tadah! This one is generously flavored with Ube.
